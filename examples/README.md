# Examples

This directory is for **illustrative placeholders only** — never real
candidates, real employers, or real vacancy postings.

## Purpose

When a template or workflow is easier to understand with a filled-in
example, put that example here rather than in
[/templates](../templates) (which describe structure only) or in
[/work](../work) (which holds real, in-progress artifacts).

## Rules for anything placed here

- Every example file must be clearly labeled at the top as an
  illustrative placeholder, e.g.:

  ```markdown
  > **Illustrative example — not a real candidate, employer, or vacancy.**
  ```

- Company names, candidate names, and vacancy details must be obviously
  fictional (avoid anything that could be mistaken for a real
  organization or person).
- Examples should demonstrate structure and reasoning style, not stand in
  for real research or real candidate data.
- Never copy real candidate materials or real job postings into this
  directory, even anonymized — use invented content instead.

## What does not belong here

Real, in-progress work belongs in [/work](../work). Real candidate source
materials belong in [/input](../input). Nothing with real personal or
company data should ever be placed in this directory.
