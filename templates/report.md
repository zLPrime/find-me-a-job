# Template: Report

> Structural template only. Reports summarize state for the candidate;
> they do not introduce new facts or judgments not already recorded
> elsewhere.

```markdown
# Job Search Report — <date or period covered>

Prepared by: reporting-agent
Status: draft | delivered

## Summary

<Short overview of progress since the last report.>

## Pipeline Status

- Employers discovered: <count, with reference to employer artifacts>
- Employers evaluated: <count>
- Vacancies discovered: <count>
- Vacancies matched: <count>
- Applications in progress: <count>
- Applications submitted: <count>

## Highlights

- <notable matches, strong opportunities, or employer findings worth the
  candidate's attention>

## Concerns / Blockers

- <anything stalled, ambiguous, or requiring candidate input>

## Recommended Next Actions

- <concrete, candidate-facing next steps>

## Appendix: Linked Artifacts

- <references to employer, vacancy, match, and application artifacts
  covered by this report>
```
