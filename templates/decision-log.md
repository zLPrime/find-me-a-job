# Template: Decision Log Entry

> Structural template for a single consequential decision. Distinct from
> [journal/decision-log.md](../journal/decision-log.md), which is a
> running chronological log for the whole process — this template is the
> structure each entry there (or on an individual artifact) should follow.

```markdown
## Decision: <short title, e.g., "Match: Senior Analyst role at Employer X">

Date: <date>
Made by: <agent name>
Decision type: match | rejection | employer evaluation | prioritization |
  other

### Decision

<What was decided, stated plainly.>

### Supporting Factors

- <evidence/reasoning in favor>

### Opposing Factors

- <evidence/reasoning against>

### Unknowns / Unconfirmed Information

- <what remains uncertain and how it affected the decision>

### Confidence

- Level: high | medium | low
- Rationale for confidence level:

### Candidate Preferences Considered

- <which stated preferences were weighed, and how>

### Linked Artifacts

- <employer, vacancy, candidate profile version, etc.>

### Revisit Trigger

- <what new information, if it appeared, should cause this decision to be
  re-examined>
```
